Entry Requirements

For A-Levels

You Need At Least upto 4 GCSE Qualifications that are a Grade 4 or Above in GCSE Mathematics and English Langauge. The Only Exception for Re-Sits will be if you have a Grade 3 in GCSE Mathematics or English Language.

For AS-Level Science, Computer Science and Maths We Accept Students with a Grade 6 or Above in GCSE Maths and in Triple or Combined Science Higher.

Note: GCSE English Literature Subjects is not Accepted.

For Vocational/BTEC Courses

Minimum Grade Reqiurement are At Least a Grade 3 or Above for Those Who are enrolling for BTEC Level 3 Courses and will be Offered a Re-Sit in Either GCSE English Language or Maths.

We Offer for a Single GCSE a Merit or Above for Those Who Have Completed the BTEC Level 2 Qualification.

For Enquiries, Please Contact Us if your Qualifcation is Not Available.
