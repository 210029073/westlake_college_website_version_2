Special Education Needs and Disabilities

We Offer a One-To-One Special Education Needs Support

At Westlake College, Support is an important step that we always prioritise for our students, if you need a little help to achieve your best. We have a range of qualified staff including communication support officers, dyslexia tutors, learner support assistants, and in-class teaching assistants.

Our level of expertise allows us to anticipate the adjustments required to promote an inclusive learning experience for all.
