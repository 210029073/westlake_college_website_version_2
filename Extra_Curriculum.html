Extra Curriculum Activities

Coding Club

Coding Club is a fun way to learn the basics of computer programming, gain problem solving and by making friends.

Throughout the Coding Club sessions students will therefore learn how to write code for exciting games.

The Coding Club is Open to everyone who wishes to pursue an IT-Career For the Future.

Gaming Club

Gaming club is an opportunity for students to socialize and have fun together based on their hobby of playing video games.

Students will have an opportunity to play multi and single player games, and spend time discussing gaming strategies and preferences.

You must be signed up for the club to attend.

Martial Arts Club

As part of the Fitness Club we feature a Martial Arts of which will improve the student’s coordination and mental focus as well as physical agility and power, making them a perfect mind body leisure activity.

For untrained members, martial arts can be intimidating to join in with, however now there are trending fighting styles which reject hierarchical (and excluding) belting systems, making them suitable for all levels to try.

The Martial Arts Club is Open to Everyone that seeks determination and have the passion to work hard and being mentally focused.
