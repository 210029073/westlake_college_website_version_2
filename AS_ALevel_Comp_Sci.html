AS/A Level in Computer Science

Overview

A Level pathways are designed to prepare you for higher education and beyond.

This course will enable learners to develop a broad range of computational skills in the areas of programming, system development, computer architecture, data, communication and applications.

The Course Materials will Cover 70% of Written Assessments and 30% of Onscreen-Based Assessments

Exam Board: WJEC

Year 1 - AS-Level in Computer Science

  • Component 1: The Fundementals of Computer Science
  • Written Assessments: 2Hrs Paper-Based Exam
  • Component 2: Practical Programming to Solve Problems
  • OnScreen Exams: 2.15Hrs OnScreen-Based Exam

Year 2 - A-Level in Computer Science

  • Component 3: Programming and System Development
  • Component 4: Computer Architecture, Data Communication and Applications
  • Component 5: Programmed Solution to a Problem

Entry Requirements

  • Five GCSE Grades at 4 or Above including GCSE English Language
  • A Grade 6 or Above in Mathematics

Please Note We Do Not Accept GCSE English Literature for an English Qualification
